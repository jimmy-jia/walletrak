What is walletrak?

Walletrak is a safe, responsible way to keep track of your finances. We don't need to connect to your bank, we don't need your date of birth, we don't even need to know your name. We believe in an unobtrusive guiding hand is one of the best ways to achieve financial stability and awareness without risking a thing.

Track your expenses

With walletrak's simple and intuitive tracking application, you will be sure to never go over budget again.

Keep tabs on everything

You'll never have to worry about remembering how much you owe, or how much someone owes you!

See how you spend

With our analytics tools, you can examine and pick through your every habit and expense.
